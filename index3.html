I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in The Nymphalidae Systematics Group (NSG), at the Laboratory of Genetics, Department of Biology, University of Turku, working with Niklas Wahlberg.

I am working on the diversification of species in Lepidoptera.

I am a Subject Editor of the taxonomical journal Zookeys (dealing with manuscripts on butterflies) and also an Associate Editor for the Revista peruana de Biología.

I am also the web application developer in our group. I use MySQL, add some PHP and spice it up with a little bit of AJAX to make our Voucher database.
Our database, VoSeq, is open source and you can download it for free. See more info.
